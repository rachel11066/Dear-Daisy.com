Types of Candle Scents

There are many types of scants that gives out different benefits!

1. Floral

Floral scented candles are a great choice in the summer months. These scents freshen the air and can help you forget about the summer heat.
If you love fresh flower arrangements burning a floral candle can be a good substitute. Floral scents are one of the most popular choices in candles.
These are soft, relaxing, romantic, and calming to the mind. Floral candles work well on bedside tables, master bathrooms, and for special occasions such as anniversaries.

Signature notes you'll find in floral blends include:

  • Rose
  • Jasmine
  • Lavender
  • Geranium
  • Ylang-ylang
  • Lilac
  • Gardenia

2. Citrus

Citrus scents are mood-lifting and energizing.
Candles with citrus scents work well in kitchens, living areas, game rooms, home offices, and hallways.
These scents are typically thought of in the summer months, but lighting a citrus candle in the winter can give you an instant mood boost and brighten your day.

Signature notes found in citrus blends include:

  • Lime
  • Lemon
  • Orange
  • Pineapple

3. Fruity

Fruity scents are very pleasing. These scents are associated with happiness and can stimulate the mood.
Fruit scents work well in the kitchen of your home.

Signature notes found in fruity blends include:

  • Apple
  • Pear
  • Apricot
  • Peach
  • Cherry
  • Strawberry

4. Woody

Woody scents are similar to what you'd notice if you were outdoors.
Smokey, woodland scents will make you feel grounded and at peace. These scents are warm and inviting.
Woody scents are perfect for highlighting an intimate dinner or an evening or curling up with a good book.

Signature notes found in woody blends include:

  • Patchouli
  • Cedarwood
  • Juniper
  • Sandalwood
  • Spruce
  • Fir
  • Cypress
  • Pine
  • Balsam

5. Earthy

Earthy scents embody the feeling of walking outdoors in the forest or the smell after a rainstorm.
These scents tend to be encouraging, balancing, strengthening, and centering.
Choose an earthy scent when you feel the need for calm and grounding.

Signature notes found in earthy blends include:

  • Cedarwood
  • Frankincense
  • Ginger
  • Sandalwood
  • Myrrh
  • Patchouli
  • Cypress
  • Balsam

6. Fresh

Fresh candle scents include herbal, citrus, and clean aromas.
They will make you feel fresh and invigorated. These scents can create an uplifting and energizing environment.
Fresh candles work well in bedrooms, bathrooms, dressing rooms, and walk-in wardrobes.

Signature notes found in fresh scents include:

  • Lime
  • Sage
  • Bergamot
  • Grapefruit
  • Lemongrass
  • Eucalyptus
  • Basil
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme

7. Sweet and Spicy

Sweet and spicy scents are reminiscent of fall.
These scents provide a cozy warm atmosphere. These candles work well in your main living areas.

Signature notes found in sweet and spicy blends include:

  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Anise
  • Honey
  • Sugarcane
  • Clove
  • Cardamom
  • Pepper
  • Amber

8. Exotic

Exotic scents are warm and welcoming.
These scents bring thoughts of indulgence and comfort.
Candles that are exotically scented often contain fragrances associated with yummy foods such as vanilla, cinnamon, and caramel.

Signature exotic scent notes include:

  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Chai
  • Coffee
  • Frankincense
  • Honey
  • Rosewood
  • Nutmeg
  • Clove
  • Balsam
  • Ginger
  • Caramel

9. Masculine


Masculine candles will have a bold, unique scent.
They are perfect in a study or man cave. These scents may have unique scents like tobacco, campfire smells, or whiskey.

Signature notes in masculine scents include:

  • Cedarwood
  • Sandalwood
  • Fir
  • Pine
  • Patchouli
  • Bergamot
  • Musk
  • Vanilla
  • Amber
  • Tobacco

I hope this can help you find the scent that is just for you!
